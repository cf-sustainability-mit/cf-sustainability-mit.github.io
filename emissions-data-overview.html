Emissions Data Overview

Through the Tableau storyboard, you can explore how companies in the Fall Career Fair rank relative to each other & see their carbon intensity data. Keep in mind that decreasing emissions is only one way for a company to be sustainable. Students are encouraged to ask companies questions about different sustainability positions as well as other sustainability efforts. Last updated 7/28/21.


If you would like to look at our data here are the 2 csv files we use: accumulated_data & intensities. We omitted some data due to constraints involving the fact that we cannot display past year's data for carbon emissions.
